What is Personal Information?

Personal information refers to any data that identifies an individual. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, contact details, payment information, and other identifiers such as IP addresses. Anonymous or aggregated data, where identities are not disclosed, are not considered personal information.

What Personal Information Do We Collect?

We may collect, use, store, and process the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Data: Name, title, marital status, date of birth, nationality.
  • Contact Data: Address, email, phone number.
  • Financial Data: Bank account and payment card details.
  • Transaction Data: Records of bookings and purchases.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, login data.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the Site and services.
  • Marketing and Communications Data: Preferences for receiving promotional materials.
  • In some cases, we may also collect special categories of personal data, such as dietary preferences, for providing personalized hospitality services, subject to your consent.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process bookings, reservations, and payments.
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Personalization: To enhance your experience with tailored services.
  • Marketing: To send promotional materials about our offerings and special deals (with your consent).
  • Compliance: To adhere to legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services and offerings.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our obligations under a booking or service agreement.
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly agreed to receive marketing communications or provide sensitive data.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, manage our business, and safeguard our operations.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Data encryption for sensitive information.
